FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.

What is the purpose of this website?

The purpose of this website is to provide information and resources for nutrition and help users track their calorie and macro intake

How can I track my calorie and macro intake?

You can track your calorie and macro intake by using our calorie tracker page. Simply input the foods and quantities you consume, and the tracker will calculate your total calorie and macro intake.

What is BMI?

BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It is a measurement that uses your height and weight to determine if you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.
